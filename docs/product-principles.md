# Unspool Product Principles

## 1. Relief Before Productivity

The writing session should feel like unloading pressure, not managing tasks.

Do not turn the main page into a dashboard.

## 2. Raw Is Valuable

The raw page is the product.

Do not over-process the writing before it is saved. Agents and second-brain systems can find patterns later.

## 3. Local First

Writing should stay on the user's Mac by default.

No account, sync, analytics, or external AI call should be required for the core flow.

## 4. One Daily Door

The app should open straight to today's page.

Anything else should be secondary and quiet.

## 5. No Fake Coaching

Unspool should not diagnose, moralize, score, or pretend to know the user's life.

If reflection features return, they should be optional and clearly separate from the writing ritual.

## 6. Keep The Name Human

Unspool means unwinding what is wound up.

Copy should be short, plain, and calm.
